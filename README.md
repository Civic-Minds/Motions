# Motions

A civic-intelligence engine that surfaces how Toronto city council votes, per-motion, in plain language.

## Problem

City council makes decisions that affect your rent, your commute, your neighbourhood — but following what's actually happening requires wading through thousands of pages of records most people will never read. Motions makes that legible.

## Features

- **Motion Pages**: Full vote breakdowns per motion — YES/NO split, sub-votes, shareable URLs.
- **Councillor Profiles**: Attendance, voting record, and head-to-head comparison between any two councillors.
- **Significance Scoring**: AI-assigned 0–100 scores surface what actually matters — major policy vs. routine procedural items — instead of keyword-matching.
- **Ward Maps**: Geographic context for votes, plus a personalized "Your Ward" view.
- **Meeting Pages**: Browse full council and committee meetings and every motion voted on that day.
- **Search**: Fast fuzzy search across the full motion history.

## Stack
- **AI**: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
- **Frontend**: React 19, Vite 7, Tailwind CSS v4, Framer Motion
- **Maps**: Leaflet, react-leaflet
- **Data**: Toronto Open Data
- **Deployment**: Vercel
